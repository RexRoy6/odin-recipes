Quesadilla Recipe

Typical Mexican Quesadilla

Queso = cheese ,tor-tilla= tortilla

A traditional quesadilla is made up of a good flour tortilla and a good smooth clear cheese.

Ingredients

  • flour tortillas
  • cheese 100 gr

Instructions

  1. bring a pan to medium heat
  2. place 1 torilla on the pan til starts to inflate
  3. while the tortilla is getting warm , grate your cheese (the whole of it)
  4. place your grated cheese on the warm tortilla and cover with the other tortilla
  5. flip the tortilla a couple of times until the cheese starts to melt
  6. eat
